Oaths

Oath of Genesis

I have taken the trials, I have given my best. Now I wear the helmet of light, and bear the robes of wisdom. Though I have completed my trial, my work has just begun. I am a new beginning of an end. I am a prodigy. I am a Temple Guard.

Oath of Loyalty

I will be loyal to the Temple Guard. I will never dispute an order, unless it contradicts our beliefs. I pledge my service, and life, to the Temple Guard when necessary. I will never betray, or sell out my brothers and sisters. My loyalty unbroken is priceless.

Oath of Silence

I will speak only when necessary, I will never complain. I will only speak freely when off-duty, though still little will I speak. For a moment of silence, is for eternity. I will never speak out of terms, from this day forth. I conceal my identity and remain silent, for all I must do is defend and serve others. Silently.
