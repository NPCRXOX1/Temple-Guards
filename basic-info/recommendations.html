Recommendations and Special Tasks

Recommendations

Recommendations are earned by Temple Guards who perform well at events, complete special tasks, or otherwise catch the attention of a Warden(+). Recommendations will not carry over upon ranking up.

Wardens Here are some examples of how to obtain a recommendation:

  • Passing a faces and formations training without making a single error.
  • Winning against the host in a Combat Training.
  • Guarding the temple with a Warden(+) and exhibiting combat skills.

Special Tasks

Special Tasks are designed to help Temple Guards earn recommendations by receiving a task from a Captain(+). A Temple Guard may request one Special Task per rank. The Captain(+) will determine what the task will be, and the difficulty of the task will increase based on the rank of the individual requesting it.

An example of a special task would be obtaining 20 kills during a Temple Guard patrol.
