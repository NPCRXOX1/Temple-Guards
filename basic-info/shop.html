Shop

System

The Temple Guard shop is a system where you may gain points for participating in Temple Guard activities to spend on privileges. Completing tasks will award Temple Guards with corresponding points. Points need to be requested by guards with the correct format in ⁠》point-requesting to be valid. Shop points reset every two months.

  • Attending an event | 5 points.
  • Co-hosting an event | 5 points.
  • Hosting an event | 5 points.
  • Guarding (Points per 10 minutes) | 1 point.
  • Winning Tournament of the Guard | 20 points.
  • Participating in a duel | 5 points.
  • Winning a duel | 5 points.
  • Answering QOTD | 1 point.
  • Winning G/HR/WoTW | 15 points.

Current Items

Shop items rotate every month and are exclusive to those who purchase them within this time frame. The items for April 2024 include:

  • Discord Nitro Premium [300 points] Temple Guards may receive Discord Nitro upon exchanging their points.
  • 100 Robux [200 Points] Temple Guards may receive 100 Robux upon exchanging their points. Must join the funding group.
  • Requirement Voucher [135 Points] Temple Guards may skip one prerequisite for the next rank, excluding Votes, Warden Evaluation, Induction Ceremony, and Sentry Duels.
  • Custom Discord Roles [130 points] Temple Guard may have a custom name, role color, and emblem within the Discord server.
  • Saber Color Privileges [100 Points] Temple Guards may use any shade of yellow or orange as a saber color.
