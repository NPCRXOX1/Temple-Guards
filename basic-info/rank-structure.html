Rank Structure

Low Ranks

Pledge

  • Pass a Temple Guard Tryout.
  • Get handpicked by a Commander(+).

Student

  • Obtain one recommendation from a Warden(+).
  • Pass one Basic Training.
  • Pass one Patrol.

Middle Ranks

Guardsman

  • Be a Student for at least two days.
  • Obtain two recommendations from Warden(+). (One must be from a Faces & Formations Training).
  • Pass one of each event-type. (Combat Training, Faces & Formations Training, Basic Training, and Patrol).
  • Pass an Induction Ceremony hosted by a Captain(+).

Sentry

  • Be a Guardsman for at least four days.
  • Accumulate two hours of guarding time displayed in duty-logs as a Guardsman.
  • Pass five events as a Guardsman. (One must be a Patrol).
  • Obtain three recommendations from a Warden(+) as a Guardsman.
  • Play five duels (win or lose) within the Dueling System.

Advanced Ranks

Vanguard

  • Be a Sentry for at least four days.
  • Have a total of three hours on-duty logged in #ã€‹activity-logs-tg as a Sentry.
  • Co-host one Basic Training and one Patrol.
  • Obtain three recommendations from a Warden(+) as a Sentry.

Warden

  • Be a Vanguard for at least seven days.
  • Co-Host four events as a Vanguard.
  • Obtain four recommendations from a Warden(+).
  • Reach Intermediate rank within the Dueling System.
  • Must not have obtained an infraction 2 weeks prior to being promoted.
  • Pass a Warden Evaluation.

High Ranks & High Command

  • Captain, Commander, Overseer
    • Pass a Ranking Vote.
    • Be handpicked by the CoS.
  • Chief of Security
    • Voted into position by the Jedi Council.
    • Be handpicked by the Grandmaster.

Retiree Roles

  • Temple Elder
    • Retire as Chief of Security (Temple Elders are eligible to the Vanguard rank as a retirement rank).
  • Fallen Overseer
    • Honorably retire as an Overseer.
    • Successfully pass a Ranking Vote.
  • Retired Officer
    • Honorably retire as a Captain(+).
    • Successfully pass a Ranking Vote.

Honor Roles

  • TG Champion
    • Win TOTG (Tournament Of The Guard).
    • Ability to use Orange Crystal. May use DC Orange if Warden(+).
    • Can change role color and name.
  • Elite Guard
    • Win a Special Event.
    • Ability to use Yellow Crystal.
    • Can change role color and name.
