Rules & Punishments

Rules

  • Be respectful to all members.
  • Follow the instructions of higher ranks.
  • Participate actively in events and patrols.
  • Use appropriate language in all communications.
  • Follow all in-game and Discord guidelines.

Punishments

  • Minor Infractions:
    • First Offense: Verbal Warning.
    • Second Offense: Written Warning.
  • Moderate Infractions:
    • First Offense: Temporary Suspension.
    • Second Offense: Extended Suspension.
  • Severe Infractions:
    • First Offense: Permanent Ban.
    • Appeal Process: Eligible after six months for reconsideration.
