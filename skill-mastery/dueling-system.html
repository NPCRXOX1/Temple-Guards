Dueling System

Rank Progression

Progression through the ranks in the Dueling System is based on the number of duels won against opponents of varying ranks. The ranks and their requirements are as follows:

Rank One

Novice
Starting rank.

Rank Two

Intermediate
Win 3 duels against a Novice(+).

Rank Three

Pro
Win 5 duels against an Intermediate(+).
Win a duel against a Pro.

Rank Four

Champion
Win the Tournament of the Guard (TOTG).
OR defeat the standing Champion.
