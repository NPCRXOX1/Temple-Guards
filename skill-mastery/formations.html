Formations

What is a Formation?

A formation refers to the arrangement of Temple Guards during a fight, patrol, or event. It is a structured layout designed to enhance the effectiveness, efficiency, and coordination of Temple Guards during their operations. Temple Guards are expected to fall into formation when a Warden(+) orders. Formations must start with an uppercase letter and end with punctuation either in the form of a '.' or '!' to be valid. The following are all official formations within the Temple Guard division:

  • Shoulder-to-shoulder with other Temple Guards while facing the commander.

Specific Rules

  • If there is a 'Prepare, Kick, March!' sequence, if 'March,' 'prepare,' or 'kick!' are improper, the sequence is invalid.
  • 'March!' is only broken by 'Halt!'
  • 'Inverse' is a tag that is applicable to the wing and wedge commands. It cannot be applied to walls, shoulder to shoulder, single file line, double column, box, or orbis commands.
  • If a command is followed by an individual's name or rank, e.g., 'Box, KillerAureus.' Temple Guards will box around that individual rather than the host.
  • If the host moves after calling a formation, Temple Guards will be expected to follow the host and re-enter formation in alignment with the host.
