Jail System

Regulations

Follow these rules to ensure proper use of the jail system:

  • Guardsman(+) are authorized to jail players.
  • Before jailing a player, they must have been warned at least twice. This can be done by saying 'Disperse!' in chat or using the command :warn <player> <reason>.
  • If a Temple Guard jails a player, log the action in #ã€‹jail-logs.
  • Misuse of the jail system will result in a strike.

Offenses

The following in-game offenses, as stated by the Jedi Order codex, may result in jail time:

  • Entering the Jedi Council chambers without proper authorization: 5 minutes
  • Disturbing Temple Guards: 5 minutes
  • Spawn Camping: 5 minutes
  • Disturbing Temple Guards (after being warned): 5 minutes
  • Attempted murder of Temple Guards: 5 minutes
  • Successful murder of Temple Guards: 10 minutes
  • Shadow Blocking: 5 minutes
  • Spawn Abuse: 5 minutes
  • Saber + Gun: 5 minutes
