Ilum Guarding Positions and Lines of Engagement

4 Person Formation

  • Red - Kill hostiles
  • Purple - Engage if allies are attacked
  • Green - Chase in these areas if attacked first
  • Orange - Guarding limit in casual lobby
  • Black - Punished if passed

Rules for Engagement

  • Temple Guards may not engage an enemy unless the hostile has passed the Kill on Sight line or has directly attacked a Jedi or a Temple Guard while close to the line.
  • Temple Guards may chase an opponent who attacked first up to a certain point. See the figure above for more information.
  • Temple Guards may not attack innocent players on the hostile team if they are exploring, unless they pass the Kill on Sight line.
  • If a Host is commanding, Temple Guards may engage any non-neutral hostile who is within engagement boundaries.
