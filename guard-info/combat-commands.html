Combat Commands

Combat commands are crucial for directing Temple Guards during in-game scenarios. These commands must be executed promptly and accurately. Below are the valid commands:

  • Disperse: Spread out across an area. Avoid clumping together.
  • Engage (name/position): Push forward to the designated target and eliminate.
  • Fall back: Tactically retreat from the current position. Use the Temple for safety.
  • Take cover: Use the pillars and other structural content to shield from blaster fire and incoming hostiles.
  • Assist (person): Help an ally defeat a hostile.
  • Defend (position/person): Hold the position and be aggressive when hostiles engage the position.
  • Escort (person) (place): Ensure the individual reaches their destination unarmed. Often used with the formation 'Box.'
  • Regroup: Move to the speaker and await further commands.
  • Prepare: Listen for upcoming commands. Can be used with Formations, such as 'Single file line.'
  • Kick: Jump in place. Can be used with Formations, such as 'Single file line.'
  • March: Move forward in formation or follow the movements of the speaker depending on the circumstance.
  • Halt: Stop movements and wait for further instruction.
